Spider Network Whitepaper

Introduction

Spider Network is a decentralized Web3 development hub focused on empowering creators, developers, and innovators by offering tools, grants, and dApps on the Sui blockchain.

Mission

To enable permissionless innovation through community-driven governance, decentralized funding, and a self-sustaining token economy.

Token Utility

  • Governance voting via DAO
  • Yield from staking via Moonbags.io
  • Revenue share from integrated bots
  • Access to premium Spider Network tools

Staking & Yield

Users can stake $WOLF tokens through the DAO platform for 2x voting power and to earn rewards via the Moonbags protocol. These earnings are automatically compounded unless withdrawn.

Revenue Sharing

35% of bot-generated fees are distributed back to the DAO stakers. Rewards are fair, transparent, and verified on-chain.

Future Integrations

  • Web3 Trading Dashboard
  • DeFi and NFT Launchpads
  • Cross-chain bridging tools
